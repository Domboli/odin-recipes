Meatballs

Meatballs Description

Meatballs are delicious balls of meat. Eat them now.

And I mean now....

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground chuck
  • 1 egg
  • bread crumbs
  • parmesan cheese
  • garlic
  • oregano
  • parsley
  • water
  • pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Pour the pasta sauce into a pot on low heat
  2. crush the garlic cloves and mix them into the meat in a big bowl
  3. crack the egg and whisk the yolk in a bowl
  4. mix the water, breadcrumbs, parsley, and oregano together in a bowl
  5. add the 2 bowls to the meat
  6. add parmesan to the big bowl
  7. mix all the ingredients together
  8. using your hands, knead and mix the meat to tenderize it until you feel you have done enough
  9. roll the meat into small balls and remove the garlic cloves
  10. add the meatballs to the pasta sauce pot and let cook
  11. serve the meatballs straight out of the pot
