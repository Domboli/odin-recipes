Rice n' Beans

Description

Rice n' beans is yummy

I put them on my balls...

Ingredients

  • white rice
  • red beans
  • ham hock
  • oil
  • tomato sauce
  • sofrito
  • sazon
  • bacon (optional)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • water

Steps

  1. pour oil and add the ham hock to a large pot on medium low heat
  2. add sofrito, bacon, tomato sauce, sazon, and pepper and let simmer
  3. add the beans, water and salt to the pot and bring to a boil
  4. reduce the heat to low and let cook for at least an hour, stirring occasionally
  5. serve with cooked rice
